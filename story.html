About Company

The Tirupati Developer is one of the most dynamic and admired organizations in the Real Estate and Hospitality sectors in Hoogly, West Bengal. More than three decades ago, the visionary founders, Mr. Rajendra Rampal and Mr. Bishwanath Sukumar Dey established the organization with a vision to change the real estate landscape of Chinsurah.....

The Group's successfully venture into Real Estate, created a series of prestigious housing, commercial and mixed-use projects spaces at strategic locatons in Chinsurah, spanning over 20 footprints covering 5.4 lac square feet in the town.
Tirupati Developers offer high quality living at great investment points. Their commitment to quality reflects in all aspects of the development cycle including planning, construction, customer service and property management.
The Group has diversified into the development of Welcome Group to build the journey into enhanced lifestyle with XXXX Club with the commitment to build luxury lifestyle in the heart of Chinsurah. Guided by a strong vision, fuelled by enthusiasm, strengthened by a strong work force and advantaged with Hi Tech building technologies, Tirupati Developers is committed to give shape to the future of not just Hoogly, but many more cities spread across the state.

Leadership

Founders Speak

Mr. Rajendra Rampal, started his journey on a humble belief winning consumers through commitment to excellence, intelligent planning, and focused effort. His fore vision has helped the organisation grow in geometric progression and also branch out into several sectors like Hospitality & Lifestyle. Mr.Rampal believes that a entity will not prosper until and unless all strategic stakeholders within the organisation like the customers, the suppliers as well as the employees are made to grow along with the Group.
Mr. Bishwanath Sukumar Dey, has more than 22 years of experience in gems and jewellery business. He partnered into Tirupati Developers, to strenthen and accelerate the journey of the group to premiumize their residential projects. Under the direction provided by Mr. Dey, the group has shown outstanding achievements and future potential in Real Estate.
